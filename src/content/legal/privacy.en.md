# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2025

## 1. Overview

BA Torment ("Service") is an unofficial fan site for Blue Archive. This Privacy Policy explains the information collected when using the Service and how it is processed.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information
- Visit history and usage patterns (Google Analytics)
- Device information (browser type, operating system, etc.)
- IP address (anonymized)

### 2.2 Information You Provide
- Gemini API key entered when using A.R.O.N.A feature (temporarily stored in session storage, automatically deleted after 30 minutes)

## 3. Purpose of Information Use

- Providing and improving the Service
- Usage statistics analysis
- Maintaining Service stability

## 4. Third-Party Services

This Service uses the following third-party services:

- **Google Analytics** - Visit statistics analysis
- **YouTube API** - Video analysis feature
- **Google AdSense** - Advertisement display

For details on how each service handles personal information, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

## 5. Cookie Usage

This Service uses cookies to improve user experience and for statistical analysis. You can refuse cookie usage through your browser settings, but some features may be limited.

## 6. Information Retention and Deletion

- API keys: Temporarily stored in session storage, automatically deleted after 30 minutes
- Analytics data: Subject to Google Analytics data retention policy
